Our Story

in Concord.

What We Serve

Oaxacan Classics

🌮
Tacos Dorados

Fried tacos topped with queso and avocado sauce. One of the most ordered things on the menu — customers mention them in almost every review.

⭐ Must Try
🫔
Tlayuda

A large open-faced tortilla with black beans, quesillo, and your choice of meat. It's a Oaxacan staple and one of the best things on the menu.

Oaxacan Classic
🌮
Street Tacos

Chicken or beef on corn tortillas with cilantro, onion, and salsa. Simple, well-made, and exactly what you'd want from a street taco.

Fan Favorite
🍚
Rice & Meat Plates

Rice, your choice of meat, and house sauces on the side. The seasoning is one of the things customers notice most — it's good.

☕
Oaxacan Coffee

Oaxacan coffee, served hot. Customers have made it a regular stop just for the coffee alone — that says enough.

New Regular Fave
🍫
Oaxacan Chocolate Drinks

Traditional Oaxacan hot chocolate made with cacao. Not overly sweet — just a solid, warming drink that pairs well with the food.
